Notes

In service since April 2017. Stretched fuselage version of A320neo. It has a 95% airframe commonality with the A320neo. It exists in two variants A321neo and A321LR that provides extended range. Total of 3438 aircraft ordered, 334 delivered (June 2020). The new A321XLR variant will offer further increased MTOW (100t) and up to 4500NM range. The new A321XLR is expected to enter service in 2022.
